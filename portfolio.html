Kitchen Renovation Project

Client: East Point Family Home

Project Type: Opened the wall with a pass-through to the dining room, replaced the old hood with a sleek, modern upgrade, and enclosed the duct in drywall.

Timeline: 2 weeks

Materials: Solid sapele countertop

Features:

  • Custom hardwood countertop shelf
  • Trimless edges in pass-through/li>
  • Drywall enclosed duct system
  • High-quality range hood replacement

Challenges:The wall for the pass-through was a load bearing wall, so special precautions were taken to reinforce the strength of the wall where we were removiing preceding support structures.

Result: A modern, open kitchen that feels like it belongs in a magazine.
